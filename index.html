Today we`re gonna tackle some of the best battle royale game that`s been released

Why is it one of the best?

It is one of the most challenging First-Person (FPS) games out there with combined mechanics of the earlier COD`S like Modern Warfare I,II,III with the same animations and movement but different environement unlike other COD`S, Warzone takes it to a different battle royale game and plus the graphics is very nice to look at it and the details of the characters and the interesting fact is most of the characters are body modeled from real people like (Captain Price) who`s modeled by John "Mac" McAleese and (Soap Mactavish) who`s modeled by Neil Ellice.

How does it differ from other different First Person Shooter games?

It differs from other first-person shooters (FPS) primarily through its loadout system letting players buy and drop in with their own fully customized weapons, its short time-to-kill (TTK), and unique second-chance mechanics like the Gulag a 1v1 arena that allows eliminated players to fight for redeployment and its Arcade-Style movement mechanics,slide canceling and tactical sprinting and Gamemodes like Resurgence, Battle Royale, Ranked play, and Plunder.
